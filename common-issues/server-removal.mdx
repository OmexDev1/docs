## Inactivity

One of the most common reasons for Omex leaving a server is inactivty, which can occur when Omex hasn't been interacted with for a prolonged period of time.
<Tip>You can reinvite Omex by clicking [here](/invite). Your previous settings will be retained.</Tip>

## Blacklisted

If your server is caught violating Discord's & Omex's Terms of Service, it will be blacklisted, resulting in Omex leaving your server. Make sure to review the rules and guidelines to ensure compliance.
<Info>If you believe your server was wrongfully blacklisted, please contact [support](/discord).</Info>
